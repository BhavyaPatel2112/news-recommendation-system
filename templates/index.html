What makes NewsAI different

Built to understand you.

Seven intelligent systems work in concert to surface exactly the stories you'd want to read.

Adaptive Profile

Every article you read updates your taste profile in real time. The more you use it, the smarter it gets.

🔍

Semantic Search

Find articles about any topic in plain English. Type "NBA playoffs" or "federal reserve" — we understand intent, not just keywords.

Explainable Picks

Every recommendation shows exactly why it was chosen — which article you read, which keywords aligned. No black box.

One slot in every round is reserved for something outside your usual topics — so you never get stuck in a filter bubble.

Popularity Signal

Real click data from 156,965 readers boosts articles that are genuinely worth reading right now.

Negative Feedback

Articles you skip are down-weighted. Your silence is as informative as your clicks.
