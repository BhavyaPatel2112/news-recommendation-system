Understanding you
01

Adaptive Profile

Updates in real time with every click and every skip. Gets smarter the longer you use it.

Uses TF-IDF vectors averaged across your chosen articles to build a living model of your interests.
02

Negative Feedback

Articles you skip are actively down-weighted. Your silence counts just as much as your clicks.

Skipped articles push the profile vector away from those topics — not just ignored, actively avoided.
03

Preference Chart

A visual history of how your interests evolve across rounds — watch the feed learn to know you.

Stacked bar chart updates after every article you pick, showing category distribution over time.
Surfacing the best
04

Semantic Search

Search in plain English — the engine understands intent, not just keyword matches.

Cosine similarity against TF-IDF vectors across the full 51K article corpus.
05

Popularity Signal

Real click data from the MIND dataset surfaces genuinely resonant articles alongside personal picks.

Blends collaborative popularity with your individual profile score for each recommendation.
06

Diversity Mode

One slot every round is reserved for something outside your usual topics — to keep the feed surprising.

Detects your dominant subcategory and injects one article from a different subcategory each round.
